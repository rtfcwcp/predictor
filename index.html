RTFC World Cup Predictor

Runcorn Town Football Club World Cup Predictor Competition

Test your football knowledge and predict the outcome of the tournament!

  • Predict all 12 Group Winners – 2 points for each correct prediction.
  • Predict the 4 Semi-Finalists – 2 points for each correct prediction.
  • Predict the 2 Finalists – 2 points for each correct prediction.
  • Predict the World Cup Winner – 2 points for a correct prediction.
  • Predict the Golden Boot Winner – 2 points for a correct prediction.
  • Predict the First Team to Receive a Red Card – 2 points for a correct prediction.

Tie-Breaker

Predict the total number of goals scored throughout the tournament. The entrant whose prediction is closest to the actual total will be ranked higher in the event of a tie on points.

If entrants remain tied after the tie-breaker, the winner will be decided by a random draw conducted by Runcorn Town Football Club.

Entry Details

Entry costs £5.00 plus a £0.13 administration fee for payment processing.

50% of all entry fees received will be allocated to the prize fund, with the remaining 50% supporting Runcorn Town Football Club. Prize money will be paid directly by Runcorn Town Football Club.
